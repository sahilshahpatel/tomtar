Welcome to the world of Tomtar

Tomtar: A Long Way Home is a cooperative puzzle platformer designed to play with siblings and friends. The game tells the story of siblings Britt and Nils as they are accidentally taken and dropped off in a new house. The tomtar must work together and use their special powers to learn about their whereabouts, evade detection by the humans, and make it back home.
Designed for and around cooperative play. An ideal experience for siblings and friends.
Put your brain to the test and use your special abilities to complete puzzles and find your way back home.
Race your way through action sequences as you evade detection by the humans.
